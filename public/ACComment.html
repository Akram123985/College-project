FAQs

Have a look at the most frequently asked questions:

Digital literacy is the ability to use digital technology, communication tools, and the internet effectively and responsibly.
Cybersecurity protects personal data, financial information, and digital identities from theft, fraud, and cyber threats.
Examples include using search engines effectively, understanding how to evaluate online information, using email safely, and managing your digital identity.
Netiquette refers to respectful and appropriate behavior when communicating or interacting online, like being polite in comments and avoiding spreading misinformation.
Protecting personal information helps prevent identity theft, scams, and unauthorized access to your accounts.
